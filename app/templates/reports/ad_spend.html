How ad credits affect the P&L

  • Gross Ad Spend is what TikTok charges (Shop Ads affiliate commission + GMV Max from the Cost export) — unchanged from before.
  • Ad Credits are amounts you manually enter here when TikTok grants you promotional credits that should reduce your reported spend.
  • Net Ad Spend = Gross − Credits. This is the figure that flows into the P&L Net Profit calculation and into ROAS.
  • On the P&L, you'll see an indented Less: Ad Credits row beneath the ad-spend lines when a credit exists for that month — so Gross and Credits remain visible separately for reconciliation.
  • Enter $0 (or leave blank) to clear a credit; the row is removed entirely.

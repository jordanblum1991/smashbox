How ad credits affect the P&L

  • Gross Ad Spend is what TikTok charges (Shop Ads affiliate commission + GMV Max from the Cost export) — unchanged from before.
  • Ad Credits are amounts you manually enter here when TikTok grants you promotional credits that should reduce your reported spend. Each credit is attributed to a specific applied date — that's the date the P&L windows it into.
  • Net Ad Spend = Gross − Credits. This is the figure that flows into the P&L Net Profit calculation and into ROAS.
  • On the P&L, you'll see an indented Less: Ad Credits row beneath the ad-spend lines whenever the period contains a credit's applied date. A custom date range only includes credits whose date falls inside [start, end).
  • Saving any amount — including $0 — records a confirmed entry that persists across reloads. A confirmed $0 shows as "✓ Saved — $0, no credit" and is distinct from a never-touched month ("— not entered"). There is no separate "clear" action; once saved, the entry is sticky.

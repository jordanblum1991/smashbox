How are the discount lines calculated?

The seller-funded discount is split at the line-item level using the Post-TikTok Discount Price (gross product sales minus TikTok's platform discount) as the base. Then line-level splits are summed to the order, and orders to the month.

  1. Start with Gross Product Sales for the line.
  2. Subtract TikTok's platform discount → Post-TikTok Discount Price.
  3. Outlandish funds the first 10% of that price: Outlandish Max = Post-TikTok Price × 10%.
  4. Outlandish-Funded = MIN(Total Seller-Funded Discount, Outlandish Max).
  5. Smashbox-Funded = Total Seller-Funded Discount − Outlandish-Funded.

Invariant: Outlandish + Smashbox always equals TikTok's reported seller-funded total — exactly, to the cent, with no rounding drift. If Outlandish + Smashbox exceeds 30% of the line's Post-TikTok Price, the order is flagged on the Reconciliation page for investigation. See app/rules/seller_funded_split.py.
